Resources

This page contains a few resources that I hope will be helpful for students applying to graduate school, undergraduate research opportunities, NSERC scholarships, and more. The statements provided below were successful at the time of application, but I should note that graduate school admissions in particular have become increasingly competitive since I applied! Nevertheless, I hope these examples statements and applications will be useful, and I ask that you please use them as a reference only.

I also really enjoy helping students with their applications, so if you ever need any advice or would like any feedback, please feel free to contact me at emily.deibert@noirlab.edu.
