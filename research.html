Research

Observations of exoplanet atmospheres in the optical and near-infrared.

My PhD thesis focused on observations of exoplanet atmospheres using high-resolution optical and near-infrared observations from high-resolution ground-based telescopes. I have published three first-author studies on the atmospheres of hot Jupiters and super-Earths, and am a Co-Investigator of a Gemini Large and Long Program (the ExoGemS survey) which aims to characterize more than 40 transiting exoplanet atmospheres over the next several years.
